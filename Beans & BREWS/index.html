About the cafe

Our Story

At Beans & Brews Café, coffee isn’t just a drink — it’s an experience. We started our journey with a simple dream: to create a space where the aroma of freshly ground beans and the warmth of great conversations meet. Every brew is made from ethically sourced beans, roasted to perfection and brewed with passion. Pair your favorite cup with our handcrafted snacks, cozy interiors, and soft music — the perfect recipe for your daily retreat.



At Beans & Brews, we believe coffee is more than just a beverage — it’s a moment of peace in a busy day. Every detail, from the selection of our beans to the way we serve your cup, is done with mindfulness and care. We aim to create not just the best coffee, but the best experience — one that warms your heart as much as your mug.




"Whether you’re a coffee lover, a student, or just someone who enjoys peaceful moments", Beans & Brews welcomes you to sip, savor, and smile.

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Address: 15 Adr street, 5015, NY
